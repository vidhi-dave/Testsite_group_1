Information on doses for drugs licensed for use in animals should be obtained from the IVS or the manufacturer’s data sheet. The information here is intended as a guide only. Remember, unlicensed drugs are given on your responsibility. Remember withholding times in food animals too. The list of drugs available in NZ (and their price!) is subject to change. ketoconazole

imidazole antifungal

indications

systemic candidiasis, dermatophyte infecions

side effects

hepatotoxicity (inhibits cytochrome P450), anorexia

interactions

prolongs action ofmany drugs metabolised by CYP

dose

dog and cat 10mg/kg daily po

ketoprofen

NSAID

indications

inflammation, pain

dose

dog & cat 1mg/kg po daily for up to 5 days, or 2mg/kg twice daily for 2 days

cattle 3mg/kg iv or im once daily for up to 3 days; WHP meat 4d, milk 0d

horse 2.2mg/kg iv daily for up to 5 days; WHP meat 28d

pig 1 - 3mg/kg im or po daily for up to 3 days; WHP not established

Anaesthetic Circuits

There are lots of different ways of classifying circuits, this is one useful classification. There can be lots of confusing minor variations!

Semi-closed circuits

These usually do not involve the animal rebreathing gas, but this depends on how they are used.

  • Mapleson A
  • Mapleson D
  • Mapleson E

Closed circuits

These nearly always force the animal to rebreathe gas.

  • circle system
  • to and fro (Water's canister)

Open and semi-open systems are obselete and you should not come across them.

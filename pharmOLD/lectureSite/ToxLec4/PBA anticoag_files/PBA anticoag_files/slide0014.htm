ANTICOAGULANTS
Determine which product and how much was eaten
Decontaminate the animal if no clinical signs are present
Use an emetic if less than 4 hours from ingestion
Clinical signs may not appear for several days.
Animal may die suddenly from internal haemorrhage
2nd generation poisoning like Brodifacoum will cause
clotting problems for 30+ days
Vitamin K1 is the treatment of choice (oral with a fatty
meal)

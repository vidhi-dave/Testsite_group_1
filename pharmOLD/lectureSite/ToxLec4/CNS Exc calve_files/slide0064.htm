CNS - MIXED EFFECTS
LEAD - Cattle
Clinical Signs:
§   Acute poisoning (calves):
§  Sudden death or stagger, vocalise, chomping,
   eye rolling, frothing
§   Subacute (usually adult):
§   anorexia, ataxia, blindness, salivation,
   muscle tremors and hyperaesthesia
§   abdominal pain, rumen atony, constipation

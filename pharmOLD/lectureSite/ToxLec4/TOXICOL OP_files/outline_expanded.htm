ORGANOPHOSPHORUS
Induced Delayed Neuropathy
OPIDN
Mechanism of Action
Caused by inhibition of neuropathy target esterase (NTE)
Loss of myelin and axons in the spinal cord
Known Substances Causing OPIDN:
· leptofos, fenitrothion, trichlorfon, trichloronat and others

Most of these lectures are in pdf format, which requires the free Adobe Acrobat to view. They are designed to be looked at, not printed. They can be printed at up to 16 slides per page (but this is very slow on a Windoze box, use a Mac if possible). If you really must waste trees by printing stuff out, the outlines are much smaller and do not have the backgrounds or photos. Remember that printing is not a substitute for learning!
If the writing comes out black on a dark background, you need a more recent version of Acrobat (8 is the latest).
The animations may not work in all web browsers: if you have problems, use the study guide web pages.

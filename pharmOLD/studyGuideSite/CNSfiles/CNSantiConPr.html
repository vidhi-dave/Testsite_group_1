Anticonvulsants - prevention

Phenobarbitone (phenobarbital USAN) is the most commonly used drug although a variety of others have been tried. These drugs are given long term per os so chronic side effects are important. No drug gives complete control - 33% of dogs have no further fits, 33% improved, 33% not improved after phenobarbitone. Treatment should only be given if fits recur regularly (usually more than one a month).

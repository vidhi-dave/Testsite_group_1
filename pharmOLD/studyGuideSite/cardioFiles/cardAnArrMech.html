Pathogenesis of arrhythmias

Arrythmias can arise from:

delayed afterdepolarisation

excess intracellular calcium
excess adrenergic stimulation
digitalis overdose

re-entry
abnormal pacemaker
heart block


Delayed after depolarisation. If the depolarisation reaches the threshold, the cell will fire and act as an abnormal pacemaker.


Re-entry.Slow conduction through a piece of damaged muscle can lead to circular propagation of action potentials in the myocardial syncytium.

Polycythaemia

Absolute polycythaemia can occur due to:

  • Disease
  • Myeloproliferative
  • Polycythaemia vera
  • elevated EPO in response to:
  • pulmonary disease
  • high altitude
  • cardiovascular disease
  • testosterone
  • decreased PO2
  • renal neoplasia
  • hyperadrenocorticism

The treatment is to try to eliminate the cause. Relative polycythaemia is usually caused by dehydration: treatment is iv fluids.

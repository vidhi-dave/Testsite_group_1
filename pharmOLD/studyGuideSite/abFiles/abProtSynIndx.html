other drugs affecting protein synthesis

Messenger RNA, transfer RNA and ribosomal RNA are constructed on the DNA template. mRNA codes the ribosome for the amino acid sequence required for construction of a particular protein. Amino acids are then transported to the ribosome by their own particular tRNAs. The amino acid chain is then assembled by transpeptidation. Antibiotics may interfere with several of the steps in this sequence.

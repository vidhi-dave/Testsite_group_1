Antibiotic essay questions

1. Compare penicillin and oxytetracycline for treatment of mastitis in the cow.

2. Discuss the use of drugs to treat pinkeye (Moraxella bovis infection) in cattle.

3. A farmer says that his pigs have “subclinical diarrhoea” and wants some antibiotics to cure them. What advice would you give him?

4. The use of antibiotics in farm animals should be banned as the risks to consumers are too great. Discuss.

5. Discuss the reasoning behind the use of a combination of erythromycin and rifampicin to treat Rhodococcus equii infection in foals.

6. How would you treat a scabby dog which has been scratching for six months and now has pyoderma?

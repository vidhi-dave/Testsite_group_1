Ion channels

Some drugs block ion channels (usually voltage gated channels) by physically clogging up the channel, eg local anaesthetics such as lignocaine. The drug may also modulate the opening or closing of the channel, eg dihydropyridine calcium channel blockers. Ionotropic receptors are different.

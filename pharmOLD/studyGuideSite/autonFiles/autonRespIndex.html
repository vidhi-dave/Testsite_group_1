Pneumonia and pulmonary oedema can be rapidly fatal and must be treated quickly.Coughing is usually caused by infection, but may also be the first sign of pulmonary oedema or bronchoconstriction. It is usually the first thing that the animal's owner notices, and the reason the vet is called in.

Definitions


pharmacodynamics = what the drug does to the animal
pharmacokinetics = what the animal does to the drug or, the movement of drugs in the body.
There are usually four components to pharmacokinetics
absorption from site of administration
distribution around the body including to the target tissue
metabolism to something which can be excreted more easily (although some drugs are excreted unchanged)
elimination from the body, usually via the kidneys and urine

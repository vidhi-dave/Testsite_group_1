Pharmacokinetics essays

1. Describe, with examples, the possible biochemical routes for drug transformation in the liver. What is the clinical relevance of this?

2. You diagnose a flock of 50 ewes as suffering from footrot which is sensitive to penicillin. Discuss the pros and cons of the different ways of giving the drug.

3. What is meant by the term “clearance”? How may it be determined from the plasma concentration versus time plot for a single intravenous bolus injection of a drug? How is kidney disease likely to affect clearance?

4. A drug rep tries to sell you an antibiotic, “Cheapocycline”. He says it is just as good as the more reputable product you usually use, because its bioavailability is the same. What is meant by “bioavailability”? Use a clearly labelled diagram to show how it could be calculated.

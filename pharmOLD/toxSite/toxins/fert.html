Nitrates / nitrites

A variety of fertilisers are occasionally implicated in poisoning of farm animals in NZ. Urea, phosphates, basic slag and fly ash toxicity have all been recorded. Nitrogenous fertilizers (sodium nitrate, calcium nitrate, ammonium nitrate or potassium nitrate) have all been associated with nitrate/nitrite poisoning.

betamethasone

glucocorticoid

indications

inflammation, allergy, ruminant ketosis, inducrtion of parturition in cattle, shock?

side effects

the full range of steroid side effects

bromide

(potassium bromide)

anticonvulsant

side effects

sedation, polyphagia, incoordination, hind leg weakness / stiffness, personality change

Precautions

Avoid in cats

dose

dog - maintenance - 20 - 40 (- 100) mg/kg daily in divided doses

loading dose (rarely necessary) 400 - 600 mg/kg divided in four over 24 h

plasma levels

therapeutic - 0.5 - 1.5 mg/mL (6.25 - 19mM) (low) achieved after 2- 3 weeks, steady state 9 weeks

with phenobarbitone - 0.8 - 2.4 mg/mL (10 - 30mM)

toxic - very variable - mild 1.5 - 3 mg/mL (19 - 38mM), severe - over 3mg/mL (38mM) (people - can be toxic at 1mg/mL (12mM), fatal at 3.2 mg/mL (40mM))

note

best used as an adjunct to phenobarbitone

If tablets are unavailable, can be used as a 20% aqueous solution.

Essay questions

These essays should take 20 - 30 minutes each to answer.

1. How is knowledge of drug - receptor interactions relevant to clinical veterinary medicine? Illustrate your answer with examples.

2. Define the pharmacological terms affinity, efficacy and potency. Discuss each of these in relation to agonists, partial agonists and antagonists

3. What do you understand by the term “spare receptors”? How, experimentally, could you demonstrate their existence? What is their clinical importance?

4. A dog is given an anaesthetic premed containing buprenorphine, an opioid partial agonist, to prevent pain during the operation. It receives fentanyl, a full opioid agonist during the op, followed by naloxone, an opioid antagonist (to counteract the respiratory depressant side effects of the opioids). How would you control the dog’s pain post op?

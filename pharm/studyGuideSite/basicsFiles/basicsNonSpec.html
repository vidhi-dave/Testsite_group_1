Non-specific drug targets

Some drugs produce an effect by a non specific physical means, eg osmotic diuretics move through the body dragging water with them until they are excreted by the kidneys and urinated out. Radioactive iodine is sometimes used for hyperthyroidism as it is concentrated in the thyroid gland; the radiation it emits then destroys all tissue within 2 - 3mm, which hopefully is all thyroid tissue.

Most drugs, particularly older drugs, are not specific for one receptor system or even one type of action - they have lots of side effects.

Vaughan Williams classification of antiarrhythmic drugs

I sodium channel blockers (reduce excitability of conducting tissue)

Ia eg quinidine
Ib eg lignocaine
Ic eg flecainide

II β blockers (reduce automaticity) eg propranolol
III potassium channel blockers (prolong action potential) eg amiodarone
IV calcium channel blockers (block nodes & damaged muscle) eg verapamil
others
Many drugs have several effects and do not fit neatly into one class.

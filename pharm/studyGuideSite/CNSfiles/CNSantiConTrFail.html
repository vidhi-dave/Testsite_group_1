Treatment failure

Seizures occurring while an animal is taking an anticonvulsant drug do not necessarily mean that the drug is not working. The original disease may be getting worse, the dose may be too low, the animal may have developed tolerance or the owner may have forgeotten to give the drug. Monitoring plasma levels is a useful way of checking (see formulary for therapeutic plasma levels).

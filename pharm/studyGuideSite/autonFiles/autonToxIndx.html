    Autonomic toxicology

    Many poisons can affect the autonomic nervous system. Anticholinestereases such as organophosphates and carbamates are probably most serious, but some drenches such as levamisole can have significant nicotinic side effects. Many plants contain autonomic drugs, eg, deadly nightshade (atropine), thornapple (hysoscine), tobacco (nicotine) and fly agaric (muscarine).

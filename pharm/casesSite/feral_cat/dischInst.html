Discharge instructions

What instructions will you give the RSPCA Inspector?

  • Take it away and if it needs more treatment, take it somewhere else.
  • Bathe the wound with antiseptic three times daily and bring it back to have the stitches out in a week.
  • Check wound daily; if it looks inflamed or open, then bring the cat back.
  • It should be fit to release to the wild.

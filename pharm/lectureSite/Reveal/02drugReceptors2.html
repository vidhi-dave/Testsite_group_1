Drug action

  • Drugs can produce effects by binding to receptors, enzymes, carrier molecules; by blocking ion channels or by exerting a physical effect.
  • There are 4 superfamilies of receptors: ionotropic, metabotropic, kinase coupled and nuclear.
  • There may be several layers of reactions in the signal transduction system between drug binding and effect.

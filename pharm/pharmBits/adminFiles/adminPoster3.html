Posters & Videos

Poster

In order to allow the rest of the class to benefit from your work, each team will be expected to present a poster of their work and be available to answer questions about it. This is basically an abstract of your essay. Posters should consist of pages of A4 paper; one each for the title and authors, introduction, conclusions and main references, and two for the main discussion. The writing should be large enough to be read easily from a distance (at least 18 point). Do not go overboard with these posters, they should involve a minimum of extra work.


The poster presentation will be in the foyer. At least one person from each group will be expected to man their poster to answer questions about it.

Video

I would also like you to make a video to present your results. If these are suitable for family viewing, they will be posted on the CALVE site so everyone can see them.

They should be a maximum of 640 x 480 and preferably mp4 format, although I can cope with most formats. To have sensible file sizes, please keep the length less than five minutes. Feel free to be creative, but remember the object of the exercise is to get info across as succinctly as possible.

POSTERIOR PRESENTATION

  • Two hindlegs presenting.Many people call these “breech”

  • Hard to tell if calf alive or dead

  • More often dead than anterior presentation

  • Pull straight back or upwards not downwards until calf’s tail out

  • Keep your hand on tail-head of calf as pull to stop jamming on pelvis or cervix

  • Beware stream of faeces as calf pelvis exits cow!

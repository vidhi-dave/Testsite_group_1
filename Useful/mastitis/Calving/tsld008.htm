POSITION CALF

  • Calf coming forward-need ropes on 2 FORElegs (above fetlock) AND a head and calf in anterior presentation

  • Calf coming backwards -ropes on 2 HINDlegs (above fetlock) and calf lying in posterior presentation

  • This is usually the hard work part for the vet but can be boring for the owner so talk about what you’re doing -or something else!

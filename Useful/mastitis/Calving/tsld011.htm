HEAD BACK (cont)

  • If calf dead usually easier to remove head with foetotome-must be as close to calf body as possible. Cover bone with hand

  • Wire threader useful

  • Can get severed head out after body if necessary

  • Sometimes need to push calf body away or pull closer using leg ropes if can’t reach head

  • Very occasionally need rope round neck to pull closer

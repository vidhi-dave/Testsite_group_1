AFTERCARE- CALF

  • Check if alive or dead-can be in between!

  • Touch and check cornea-if cloudy calf dead

  • -if don’t blink MAY be dead

  • -if blinks immediately calf viable

  • If calf sits up and shakes head-viable

  • If calf lies flat-out gasping-much less viable

  • Excess movement of calf can be death throes!

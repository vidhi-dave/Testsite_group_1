PULLING (cont)

  • Once head of calf out stop pulling on head

  • Anterior presentation-if big calf twist pelvis of calf before it jams (hiplock) or split pelvis (foetotomy) if does jam

  • Posterior presentation-Don’t stop when calf half out-can suffocate

  • Keep your hand over head or tailhead of calf until in pelvis-stop any jamming on cervix or pelvic entrance

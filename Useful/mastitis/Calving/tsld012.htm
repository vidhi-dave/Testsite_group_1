FORELEG(S) BACK

  • Calf head can be very swollen-hard to tell if dead or alive

  • Head must go back into pelvis to get retained leg (s). Use lots of lube

  • Give epidural and then push head back in -can use “prolapse position” if cow down

  • Remove head if calf dead-use knife just behind ears (atlanto-occipital joint)-don’t cut cow! Only time rope on calf’s jaw useful!

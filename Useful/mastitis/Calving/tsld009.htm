ANTERIOR PRESENTATION

  • VERY COMMON -pulling on legs alone often ineffective if tight fit

  • HEAD ROPE applied correctly essential -never on bottom jaw!

  • Can be fixed or floating-either case head rope helps

  • Floating calves are due to uterine inertia usually milk fever or induction

  • Need head through cervix before attempt to extract calf if not 100% open

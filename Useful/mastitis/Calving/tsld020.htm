FIZZERS (ROTTEN CALVES)

  • Once dead a calf can turn into a “fizzer” in 12 hours or less and then continues to rot and fall apart

  • Often head back, breech or second twins

  • CAN’T let gas out like a balloon

  • Need HEAPS OF LUBE-use pump

  • Look big but often deceptive-gas mainly- so can use lots of traction compared to fresh calf

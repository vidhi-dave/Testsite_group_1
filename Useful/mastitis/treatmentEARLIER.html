The treatments available for mastitis can be broadly divided into those used to treat clinical mastitis during the lactation and those administered after the last milking to provide a persistent source of antibiotic in the udder during the dry period.

A variety of drugs that behave differently in the udder are incorporated into products available for dairy cattle.

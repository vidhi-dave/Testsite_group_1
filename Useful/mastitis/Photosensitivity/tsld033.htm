Treatment

  • If cow flat-out with milk fever should give some calcium first.

  • Heifer down with prolapse quite likely to have some paralysis as well.

  • If cow/heifer down put into “prolapse position”-sitting on stifles, feet behind and farmer on top if cow stroppy. IMPORTANT

  • Looking back- some xylazine may be useful in stroppy heifers

Hepatogenous Photosensitivity

  • PDA is PHYLLOERYTHRIN

  • Microbes in ruminant gut convert CHLOROPHYLL from plant diet into PHYLLOERYTHRIN which goes to liver where it is excreted in the bile.

  • Liver damage interferes with this process and Phylloerythrin builds up in tissues

  • about 14 days for skin signs to develop-check feed from 2 weeks before

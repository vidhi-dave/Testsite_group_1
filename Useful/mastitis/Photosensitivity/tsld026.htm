Taranaki Anaemia

  • One manifestation of CHRONIC HYPOMAGNESAEMIA

  • Not only found in Taranaki-most of country except Southland and Manawatu

  • Magnesium important for appetite, milk production and formation of RBCs

  • Iron not properly incorporated into haemoglobin-produces nucleated RBCs and stippling as well as non-regenerative anaemia in a classic case

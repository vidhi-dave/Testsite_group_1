Taranaki Anaemia-Presentations

  • Classic case has weight loss, decreased appetite, empty in guts,lowered milk production and might have slightly pale mucous membranes-nothing else abnormal on examination-an “off colour” cow!

  • Some also have “leatherbag” (udder oedema or “padded udder”) affecting all or part of udder

  • Some are nervous and hyperactive

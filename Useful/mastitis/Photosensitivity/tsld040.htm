Suturing- my method

  • 3-4 loops of umbilical tape or doubled skin suture down each side of vulval lips

  • Just loose enough to pass string or tape under- sometimes use forceps to pass

  • Must be placed in the border of hair and hairless vulval skin. Go fairly deep into S/C tissue.

  • Leave finger sized opening at bottom

Photosensitivity in Cattle

  • Four types depending on source of photodynamic agent (PDA).

  • 1. Primary- PDA absorbed pre-formed.

  • 2. Secondary (Hepatogenous)- PDA normally metabolised by the liver accumulates in bloodstream usually due to liver damage.

  • 3. Porphyric or congenital- PDA formed in body during aberrant pigment synthesis.

  • 4. Uncertain or unknown aetiology.

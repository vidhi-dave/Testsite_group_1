Treatment

  • GET OUT OF SUN! Covers not always good

  • Symptomatic only.Remove source-if known!

  • Skin will still peel once signs noticed

  • Sunblock cream eg FiltaBac, Zinc ointment-really only practical on udder and perineum.

  • Antihistamines, NSAIDS, corticosteroids (depending on stage of pregnancy!)-take your pick depending on case

  • Many cows need to be dried off

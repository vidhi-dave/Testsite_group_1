Mechanism

  • Necessary for photosensitivity to occur-

  • 1. PDA present in skin

  • 2. Skin exposed to certain wavelengths of sunlight

  • 3.Sunlight must be absorbed by the skin

  • PDAs in skin react on exposure to sunlight and fluoresce which causes cellular damage seen grossly as “sunburn” with ulceration and necrosis

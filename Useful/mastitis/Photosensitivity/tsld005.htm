Spring Eczema-Symptoms

  • Not always obvious symptoms-need to look and feel. Sometimes inflammed-easy to see

  • Run hands over broken coloured parts of body-should feel swelling of skin on paler parts-Friesians are good for this.

  • Check vulva and perineum and down to udder and teats (generally thinner-haired parts of body) -don’t get kicked-can be very painful cows

Treatment

  • Stage 2 - invert uterus inside cow-sounds easy but can be difficult to get right

  • - always give cow or heifer calcium even if no milk fever (S/C only)

  • - Oxytocin and antibiotics (parenteral and pessaries) always

  • - Recheck position of uterus -consider Planipart

  • - rarely suture vulva-only if membranes a problem

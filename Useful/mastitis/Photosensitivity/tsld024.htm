“Walking Milk Fever”

  • Very occasionally have seen this

  • Cow has all usual signs of milk fever-dry nose, bloated atonic gut, groaning, depression, dung dry and mucus covered- BUT cow is up and WALKING!

  • Could be confused with gut condition requiring surgery!

  • Responds rapidly to calcium (S/C) and stomach tubing if very bloated

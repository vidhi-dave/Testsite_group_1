Treatment

  • In some herds Taranaki Anaemia cows are a “tip of the iceberg” situation-should check magnesium level of herd to find out.

  • Some cows get this season after season-suggest culling.

  • Slow release magnesium products such as bullets (Rumbul, Sire Sine) or long-acting drench such as More-Mag are usually better than extra daily drenching or dusting

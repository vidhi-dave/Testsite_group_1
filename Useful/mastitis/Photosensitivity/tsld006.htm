Symptoms-more

  • Check muzzle, eyelids and ears-initially these can have subtle swelling only

  • Some cases standing in sun can be acting “nuts”-can be hard to examine safely

  • Have seen above signs even in dark red (Hereford coloured) cattle and black Angus

  • In later stages patches of sunburn are obvious with skin peeling off. Similar to people with sunburn this can LOOK bad but feel OK

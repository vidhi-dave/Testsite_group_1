Approach to Prolapses

  • Down in paddock- put halter on and attach it to something in case of sudden recovery!

  • Up in paddock- minimize movement. May need to confine in angle of gate or drive into corner made by bike, ute etc. Ropes or electric tape useful

  • DON’T suggest cow is bought up to yard- more potential damage to uterus the more the cow is shifted

Diagnosis

  • Often done on clinical signs alone

  • Can take blood samples to check for liver damage-may or may not be present.

  • Important to know if FE from herd point of view-when case at unusual time of season

  • GGT and GLDH most useful- with FE GGT higher than GLDH and vice versa

  • Histology on liver biopsy or liver from dead cow if available

Spring Eczema

  • Condition produces a photosensitive (sunburnt) bovine that is NOT due to FACIAL Eczema

  • Can occur at ANY time but often in “spring” - October/ December

  • Generally only sporadic cases-1or 2 cows per year affected on a farm and some farms may never see it

  • Varying amount of discomfort and/or illness for cow and hassle for farmer

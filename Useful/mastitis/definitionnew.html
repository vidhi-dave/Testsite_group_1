Mastitis:

Inflammation of the mammary gland resulting primarily from bacterial infection.

Clinical Mastitis:

Inflammation characterised by any visible change in the milk such as clots or a colour variation.
This may be accompanied by any of the following clinical signs: swelling, heat, pain, redness of the udder or fever.

 

Mastitis causes a significant loss of production.
